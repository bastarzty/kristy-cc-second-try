Parsons Design & Technology


I am from a design background and I'm interested in editorials, graphics, images, and interaction design that helps people.


As a designer and creator, I am passionate about crafting experiences that bridge the gap between technology and human interaction. My work explores themes of inclusivity, play, and introspection, with a particular focus on designing tools and experiences for individuals with ADHD.

Drawing from personal insights and a deep interest in experiential design, I aim to create
projects that not only engage but also empower, fostering creativity, focus, and connection.
From interactive games to thoughtful interfaces, my goal is to reimagine technology as an
empathetic and intuitive extension of the human experience.


I seek a transformation in my career that goes beyond simply branding and building visuals. I also want to embrace user experiences in my work.
